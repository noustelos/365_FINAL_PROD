Interactive Functions

✦

365orthodoxy includes simple and direct features:

• Search by Date: Select any day of the year via a calendar and see which Saints are celebrated.

• Search by Saint's Name: Instantly find when your name day is celebrated, or find the feast days of your loved ones.

• “Random Quote”: The "New Quote" function displays a random message of wisdom for the moment you need it most.

• Copy and Share: Easily copy the passage or search result to save or share with others.

• Instant Translation: Toggle between original and modern text for better understanding.

• Deeper Knowledge: Direct access to Saints' biographies (via Wikipedia).

• Modern Saints: The teachings of Saint Paisios and Saint Porphyrios.
